How to upload shop images on Apex Mechanic

Why should you care?
The reason these photos matter is because if we head over to the Apex Auto app, you can see how your profile looks to potential customers, and the photos will be the first thing they see.
Having better photos increases the likelihood that a new customer will schedule an appointment at your repair shop.
You're going to see at least a minimum of 4 photos. To upload a new photo, just click on the “Add New Image” button and select the photo you want to upload.

1. Go to account settings

From Account, you can head over into the Shop Details section
Scroll down until you see Shop Images.
You're going to see at least a minimum of 4 photos. To upload a new photo, just click on the “Add New Image” button and select the photo you want to upload.

2. Instructions to upload images

You can upload up to 10 photos.
In order to change any of the photos, just hit the pencil icon here and upload a new photo.
The new photo uploaded will replace the existing photo.
If we want to reorganize which photo is first, All you need to do is click and hold the image and drag it to the desired location.
The first image will always be the cover image.
In order to delete a photo, just hit the little trash icon, and it will give you an option whether you want to delete this image or not.
The delete option does not work for one of the first 4 images. You do have to drag it to the very bottom in order to delete the image because there has to be a minimum of four photos uploaded at any time.

3. Save changes

And once you like how your photos look, all you need to do is hit save changes, and that's going to save the changes to your Apex AutoShop profile.
