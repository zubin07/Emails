Importance of Review Management
Shops with good Google reviews are more likely to appear at the top of local search results, attracting more customers who are actively looking for services nearby.
Potential customers are more likely to choose a business with a high rating and recent positive feedback, which helps turn website visitors into actual customers.
Google reviews enhance Search Engine Optimization (SEO) rankings by providing unique, user-generated content that improves keyword optimization, making it easier for people to find your shop.

1. Manage your appointments with Apex Mechanic

One of the key benefits of using Apex Mechanic to manage your appointments is the automatic email and notification system.
When you mark a vehicle as “dropped off” or “ready for pickup,” your customer receives an email and an app notification, keeping them informed every step of the way.
Here’s where Smart Review Management comes in:

1. Automatic Thank-You Email

The customer receives a personalized thank-you email from your shop. This simple gesture goes a long way in enhancing their experience and satisfaction, all with just one click!

2. Rate Your Experience Prompt

The customer is invited to rate their experience with your shop. Here’s how it works:
  • Low Ratings (1-3 stars): If a customer leaves a low rating, you’ll be notified via email. This gives you a chance to address their concerns privately, without it being posted on Google.
  • High Ratings (4-5 stars): If the customer leaves a high rating, they’ll be prompted to also leave a review on Google Maps. They’ll be taken directly to your Google page, with their review text already copied to their clipboard—making it easy for them to paste and share.
This feature also works seamlessly within the Apex Auto app. As soon as an appointment is marked as ‘customer paid,’ the customer is prompted to rate their experience. Positive reviews will be directed to Google, helping boost your shop’s online ranking and ensuring that only positive feedback reaches your Google page.

2. How to Get Started

To start using Smart Review Management, simply create and manage your appointments through Apex Mechanic. Make sure to enter customer emails so they receive these automated notifications and emails.
